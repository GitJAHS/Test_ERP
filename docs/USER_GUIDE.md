# Enterprise ERP | User Guide

Welcome to the Enterprise ERP. Learn how to manage corporate workloads using our Material You browser system.

## 👥 Customer CRM Module
1. Navigate to **Customers (CRM)** on the sidebar nav.
2. View total spends of onboarded accounts.
3. Click **onboard Customer** to input corporate credentials, address boundaries, customer type tiers, and credit limit bounds.
4. Export directories directly by clicking **Export CSV**.

## 📦 Products & Inventory
1. Choose **Products (Inventory)** to view stock records.
2. Select **onboard SKU** to save new product IDs, barcodes, standard prices, tax rates, and warehouse locations.
3. Keep track of available levels (adjusted automatically based on issued orders).
4. Utilize category filters and search fields to quickly pinpoint low-stock items.

## 🧾 Invoicing Desks
1. Select **Invoices** to review ledgers.
2. Click **Create Invoice** to assemble a structured layout:
   - Choose clients. Their details auto-fill.
   - Click **Append Item** to insert SKUs, select sizes, and adjust quantities.
   - Enter payment amounts if immediate cash is received.
   - Apply coupon/promo codes.
   - Check stock warnings highlighted in red.
3. Print invoices locally formatted for standard desktop printers, or generate instant mail reminders.

## 📊 AI Smart Advisor
1. Access the **AI Advisor** tab for pro-active suggestions.
2. Check for low stock alerts or overdue client balances.
3. Click "Order PO" to auto-open prefilled POs with suppliers.
4. Click "Call Supplier" or "Email" to prompt automated communications.
